Biography

My background

I grew up in Hanoi, Vietnam. My family owns a business and I have ever been inspired to become an entrepreneur like my parents. I have two much elder sisters, which made me the aunt of two little babies.

My family has always stressed the importance of education on their children, and I consider myself a living proof of their Asian heritage. Nevertheless, my parents always respect their kids' decisions and are quite open-minded.

My education

I have grown up in such a disciplined yet supportive environment, which helps me strive for success and never has the opportunity to blame my procrastination on my strict parents (sadly). Currently, I am a senior majoring in Computer Science at Caldwell University. I have always been interested in business and social matters. However, after learning math and programming languages for a while, I feel like I have so much to learn from and contribute to the growing technology of the 2st century. Therefore, I have decided to choose my current major in order to grow my tech skills set, and also to prove to my Asian relatives that a girl can code :)

Future plan

I have just started with my major in Computer Science and there is so much more to explore. At the moment, I am quite interested in web development. After graduation, I want to work as a frontend software engineer, and later on a full-stack engineer, which may not come into reality in years.

I am also passionate about providing high-quality STEM education to young children, particularly those in underdeveloped and developing countries. If I have the opportunity, I would like to use my degree in computer science to do such meaningful things later in life.
